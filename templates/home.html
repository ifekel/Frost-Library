Can I download books? 

Answer. NO, you can only add a book to your shelf and the book can be read while using the site

Can I take the same book more than once? 

Answer. NO, unless the previous book collected hass missing pages taking the same book again will be allowed. Apart from that no user can take the same book more than once.

Maximum book to have in a shelf

Answer. The maximum number of books a user can have in their shelf is 5. If you need to take more books after having 5 books in your shelf you will need to return a book or 2 or the amount required.
